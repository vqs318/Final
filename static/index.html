IDE Final Project

Reddit Comment Markov Chains

Background:

We decided we wanted to use Reddit for our final project because it was something we both used and could provide a variety of data for visualization. We became interested in Markov Chains after coming across r/SubredditSimulator. The entire subreddit is generated by bots crawling the various subreddits and creating posts and comments based on Markov Chains. Markov chains are a sequence of possible events where the probability of the next event only depends on the state attained in the previous event.

The Project:

Our project uses the comments section of a chosen subreddit to produce a tree like structure that signifies which words are more likely to follow another word in a sentence. The first row of words is the top 10 most likely words to start a sentence within the specified subreddit. The following words are the 10 most likely words to occur after that specific word. Hovering over a word will show the weight of the word which is the probability that that word will be used.
